📌 Official Rules (Summary)
Academic Warning (Bachelor)
• Warning applies after completing 12 credit hours or more.
• If CGPA < 2.00 → warning at the end of any semester (except summer).
• Dismissal may occur if warnings reach 5 in 5 consecutive semesters.
Graduation Requirement (Bachelor)
• Bachelor degree is awarded after passing all courses in the study plan
• AND CGPA must be not less than 2.00 out of 4.00.
Failing Status Codes
• FC: Fail in Continuous Assessment (CA) below 30% of CA grade.
• FA: Fail due to absenteeism with no excuse.
• FF: Fail in Final Exam below 40% of Final grade.
• F: Total (CA + Final) below 50%.
Note: Fail marks are counted within semester and cumulative GPA.
